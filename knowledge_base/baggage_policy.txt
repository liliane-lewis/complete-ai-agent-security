=== AirCorp Baggage Policy ===

Carry-on baggage: 1 bag up to 10kg, max dimensions 55x40x20cm.
Checked baggage: 1 bag up to 23kg included for Gold and Platinum members.
Basic members: checked baggage charged at $30 per bag.
Excess weight: $10 per kg over the limit.
Prohibited items: liquids over 100ml, sharp objects, flammable materials.

For questions contact: baggage@aircorp.com
